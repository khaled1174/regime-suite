HMM detects latent regimes using rolling returns & vol via Viterbi-style thresholding.

Green=bull · Red=bear · Yellow=choppy
GMM fits Gaussians to (return,vol) space — each day gets a probability per regime, not a hard label.
K-Means clusters daily (return,vol) pairs by nearest centroid. Fast & deterministic.
GARCH(1,1) models time-varying conditional variance. Vol clustering: high follows high.
Ensemble majority-votes all 4 models. Higher agreement = stronger signal.
